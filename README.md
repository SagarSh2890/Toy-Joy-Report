# Toy-Joy-Report
Toy-Joy Seasonal Sales & Inventory Analysis Report
Prepared by: Sagar Sharma

Case Study Objectives --
• Identify best-selling and slow-moving products
• Understand stock levels
• Analyse seasonal demand
• Provide business insights

KPIs --
• Total Units Sold: 160K+
• Total Revenue: 4.7M+
• Inventory Turnover Rate: 0.33
• Excess Inventory: 168K

Product-Level Insights --
• Outdoor, Puzzle, Plush Toys perform best
• Some educational toys are slow-moving
• High closing inventory for some products

Category-Level Insights --
• Soft Toys, Outdoor, Action Figures lead sales
• Lower demand categories need review

Seasonal Demand Analysis --
• Peak sales in Oct–Dec
• Low sales in May–June
• Helps in planning stock

Key Findings --
• Strong seasonal trends
• Overstocking issues
• Capital blocked in excess inventory
• Category imbalance

Recommendations --
• Improve forecasting
• Reduce overstocking
• Increase festive season stock
• Discount slow-moving items
